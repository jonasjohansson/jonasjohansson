# Jonas Johansson

Creative technologist, designer, and educator based in Stockholm.

I run my own [studio](https://jonasjohansson.se) since 2007, working across interactive installations, projection mapping, audiovisual systems, and custom electronics. I frequently collaborate with [Smash Studio](https://smash.studio) as creative technologist and technical director. My work combines real-time systems, AI-based workflows, and custom-built hardware using platforms such as TouchDesigner, Unreal Engine, and ESP32.

I also initiate and lead artistic platforms and communities, including [Visualia](https://visualia.se) artist residency, [Nordic Audiovisual Artists](https://nordicaudiovisualartists.com), the lighting collective [Svartljus](https://svartljus.se), and the fulldome film festival [Dome Dreaming](https://domedreaming.com).

## Experience

- **Senior Lecturer** — Beckmans College of Design, Stockholm (2017–2025)
- **Designer** — KRAM/WEISSHAAR, Stockholm & Munich (2015–2016)
- **Industry Leader** — Hyper Island, Stockholm (2014–2022)
- **Designer** — Field, London (2014–2015)
- **Interactive Art Director** — Moment Factory, Montréal (2012–2013)
